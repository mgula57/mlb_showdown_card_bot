## Version 4.2 — User Accounts

### User Accounts

You can now create an account on Showdown Bot.

- **Sign up** with an email and password, or use **Continue with Google** for one-click sign-in
- Choose a **unique username** when you register — usernames are checked for availability in real time
- Your **account icon** lives in the top-right corner of every page. When you're signed in, it shows your initials and gives you quick access to your account
- New **Account Settings page** (`/account`) lets you:
  - View your profile (username, email, member since date)
  - Change your username at any time
  - Set your preferred **theme** (Light, Dark, or System)
  - Set your **default Showdown set** for card generation

### Privacy Policy

A privacy policy is now available at `/privacy`, covering how data is collected and used.

## Version 4.11 — 2026 Season Updates & MiLB

### Minor League (MiLB) Cards

You can now generate cards for **Minor League Baseball players**. Search for a MiLB player by name in the Custom Card Builder just like you would any MLB player. Cards are generated with the appropriate league context.

### Situational Splits

When building a custom card using the **2026 season** (MLB Stats API), you can now choose a **situational split** — for example, stats vs. left-handed pitching, vs. right-handed pitching, at home, or on the road. A new dropdown in the Custom Card Builder lets you select from available splits for the player.

### Two-Way Player Support in Leaders

The home page Leaders section now properly handles **two-way players** like Shohei Ohtani, showing their pitching and hitting lines separately.

### Multi-Year Date Ranges

When building a card with a custom date range, you can now span **multiple years** in a single range (e.g., April 2024 – September 2025).

## Version 4.1 — 2026 Opening Day
